The honest part

AI is changing the math for solo builders.

I designed and built Glean end to end, by myself. That used to mean months. AI was the force multiplier that turned it into days — not by doing the thinking for me, but by removing the drag between an idea and a working build.

  • Idea → working screen, same sitting. Design directions became real React Native UI fast enough to actually feel the product and iterate.
  • Native walls cleared in minutes. The gnarly iOS build and linker issues that used to eat days got diagnosed and fixed quickly.
  • The whole launch surface, not just code. Icon, marketing site, store assets, and copy — produced and polished without a separate sprint for each.

The taste, the product decisions, and the privacy stance are still mine. AI just collapsed the distance to shipped.

What Glean does
Scan receipts and documents into crisp, searchable PDFs.
Edit, then store straight to your Google Drive — or keep everything on your device.
Privacy by Design: on-device scanning and OCR — no servers ever see your documents.
