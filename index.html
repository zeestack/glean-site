What it does

From paper to searchable PDF, privately.

Capture, clean up, and recognize text — all on your iPhone.

Scan & clean up

Automatic edge detection and perspective correction. One‑tap Clean up removes shadows and glare, whitens the page, and sharpens text.

Searchable PDFs

On‑device text recognition turns every scan into a searchable PDF. Find and copy the text inside — no internet required.

Your storage, your choice

Every scan is saved on your device — even in the Files app. Connect Google Drive only if you want a cloud copy.

Private by design

No servers ever see your documents.

Scanning and text recognition happen entirely on your iPhone. No analytics, no tracking, no backend that touches your files. Sign in with Apple or Google, and delete your account and data anytime.
