Contact

We're here to help

Common topics

Scanning

Tap the Scan button, capture your pages, then crop, rotate, apply filters, or use “Clean up” to remove shadows and sharpen text.

Searchable PDFs

Text is recognized on your device, so your PDFs are searchable — no internet needed.

Google Drive (optional)

Connect it in the Cloud tab to also save PDFs to a folder you choose. Your scans are always kept on your device too.

Signing in

Use Sign in with Apple or Continue with Google. Drive is optional for both.

Deleting your data

Open the Account menu and choose “Delete account & data” to remove your local scans, disconnect your account, and revoke access.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy for how Glean handles your data.
